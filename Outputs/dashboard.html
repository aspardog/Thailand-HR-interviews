Dimension 0: Current Status of Fundamental Rights

Overall Assessment

0
"Improved from post-coup era, but fragile and incomplete"

Key Challenges

  • Section 112 (Lese-Majeste) - Creates chilling effect on all political expression
  • Selective Enforcement - Laws applied unevenly based on political alignment
  • Military Legacy - Influence embedded in institutions
  • Deep South Conflict - Special laws, ethnic discrimination, impunity
  • Migrant Exploitation - Fishing industry forced labor

Areas of Progress

  • Marriage Equality Act - First in Southeast Asia
  • Anti-Torture Act (2022) - New legal framework
  • Youth Engagement - 2020-2021 protests awakened generation
  • Digital Government - Thai ID tools working
  • Labor Court - Efficient, accessible

1

Key Challenges

  • Section 112 - Prosecutions for posts, shares, likes
  • Computer Crime Act - Catch-all for critics
  • No Anti-Discrimination Law - Groups left behind
